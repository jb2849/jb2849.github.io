Brief Introduction

Junchi Bin received the B.Sc. in eletrical engineering from dual-degree program of Northern Arizona University, AZ, USA and Chang'An University, Shaaxi, China in 2016. Then, he received the M.A.Sc. and Ph.D. in electrical engineering from the University of British Columbia, Kelowna, Canada, 2019 and 2023 respectively. Junchi's research interests include urban computing, computer vision and anomaly detection. Besides, he also has involoved in various industrial projects like real estate appraisal and iris identification in different companies.
