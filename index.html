Brief Introduction

Junchi Bin received the B.Sc. in eletrical engineering from dual-degree program of Northern Arizona University, AZ, USA and Chang'An University, Shaaxi, China in 2016 and the M.A.Sc. in electrical engineering from the University of British Columbia, Kelowna, Canada, 2019. Junchi Bin is currently a Ph.D. student in electrical engineering at The University of British Columbia, Kelowna, Canada, where he works under Prof. Dr. Zheng Liu. His research interests include data fusion and machine learning. Besides, he has broad skills in urban computing, computer vision and anomaly detection. Besides, he also has involoved in various industrial projects like real estate appraisal and iris identification in different companies.
